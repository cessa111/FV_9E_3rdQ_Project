HOW TO PREVENT FIRES ğŸ”¥

Unlplug all electrical items when they are not in use. Avoid overloading the electrical circuits as this could lead to an explosion from overheating, which could cause a fire.

Keep flammable items such as lighters, candles, gas tanks, matches, and more, away from children. Children playing with flammable items could lead to the child getting hurt and your house burning down.

Plan ahead. Establish escape routes and emergency plans. Familliarize yourself with your surroundings and figure out the fastest way to leave the building.

Don't leave your kitchen unattended while cooking. Kitchen fires are mostly caused by leaving whatever it is that you're cooking unattended.


When a fire starts, call your local authorities immediately. Leave all unimportant things behind and evacuate.

If your clothes get caught on fire, don't panic and remember to "Stop, Drop, and Roll"
